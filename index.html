About Me

I’m Aaron Cobbinah, a passionate graphic designer from Takoradi, Ghana, born on 16th July 1999. I completed my basic education at Believers International School in 2014, and continued my secondary education at Saint Augustine’s College, graduating in 2017. I pursued higher education at GIMPA (Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration) and completed in 2021.

My journey as a designer is driven by creativity, precision, and a strong desire to bring ideas to life through visuals.

My Vision

My ultimate goal is to gather young African developers and creatives to build the biggest and best tech company in Africa — one that can compete globally and inspire innovation across the continent.

I'm currently seeking passionate people to join this journey. Together, we can change Africa's tech future.
